Box mode

In box mode, you can drag and create boxes with the mouse

The box with === in its top and bottom border is the selected box. You can edit its contents with [ edit ]

You can drag boxes.

You can resize boxes by dragging the lower right corner.

Draw mode

In draw mode, you can add characters to the drawing.

Hold down shift and move the cursor to draw diagram lines.

You can place the cursor with the mouse and use the arrows to move.

You can drag open a selection rectangle and move it with the mouse or delete the contents with the delete key.
